Why this layout?

I created this page to fill the gap between 1999's page and 2007's page since, 2004's page and 2007's page are near identical. The similarity of those pages happened because I seemingly wrongly assumed that the technological advancement between 2004 and 2007 would cause the two to look significantly different. Now, I suppose the 2004 and 2007 pages can demonstrate that replicating every year from 1991 to the present would likely result in many pages that look similar to each other.

Anyway, I did not peruse literature to help determine the 2002 page's layout since this is not a school project anymore. I based the 2002 page on what I saw from other 2002 websites. The only consistent change that I noticed was the use of a navigation bar and box shadows.
